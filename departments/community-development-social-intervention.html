Overview

The Community Development & Social Intervention Department is dedicated to improving the quality of life in communities across Nigeria through sustainable development programs and targeted social interventions.

Key Functions

  • Develop and implement community development programs and policies
  • Coordinate social intervention initiatives for vulnerable populations
  • Facilitate community participation in development projects
  • Promote sustainable livelihood programs
  • Collaborate with local and international partners for community development
