Overview

The Finance & Accounting Department is responsible for the effective management of financial resources, ensuring transparency, accountability, and compliance with financial regulations in the Ministry's operations.

Key Functions

  • Budget preparation and implementation
  • Financial reporting and accounting
  • Treasury and cash management
  • Financial compliance and internal controls
  • Payroll and staff benefits administration
