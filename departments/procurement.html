Overview

The Procurement Department is responsible for the acquisition of goods, works, and services in accordance with the Public Procurement Act and other relevant regulations, ensuring value for money and transparency in all transactions.

Key Functions

  • Process procurement requests in line with approved thresholds
  • Maintain and update the Ministry's procurement plans
  • Coordinate tender processes and contract awards
  • Maintain a database of qualified vendors and contractors
  • Ensure compliance with public procurement regulations

Goods & Works
