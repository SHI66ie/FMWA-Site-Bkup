General Services

Ensuring operational excellence and efficient service delivery across the Ministry

Overview

The General Services Department provides essential administrative and support services to ensure the smooth operation of the Ministry and its various departments.

Key Functions

  • Facilities and office space management
  • Procurement of goods and services
  • Transport and logistics coordination
  • Records and document management
  • Maintenance of office equipment and facilities
